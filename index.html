About

Crafting Cinematic
Tamil Stories

S. Mani Arputha Raj is a multidisciplinary creative force from Dindigul District, Tamil Nadu. His work spans the full arc of storytelling — from the written word to the composed note, from the poet's pen to the director's lens.

As the founder of Life Studios and Life Productions, he has built a creative home that nurtures Tamil cinema, music, and poetry. His YouTube channel "Life Studios" brings these creations to audiences who believe stories have the power to change lives.

Every frame, every lyric, every melody carries the fingerprint of his native soil — the rhythms of Dindigul, the depth of Tamil culture, and the universal language of human emotion.
